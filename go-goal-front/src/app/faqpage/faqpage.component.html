Account

What info do I need to set up my account?

To create an account, all you need to provide is your name, email, and a preferred username and password. Go Goal takes no other personal data to set up an account, and you can start making goals and making friends immediately after!

How do I login?

To login, just go to the login page and enter you Email and Password!

Can I change any of my info?

Yes! If you ever want to change any of your personal info, simply login and go to Account Settings either from your profile page or the menu. You can change your name, email, username, and password here.

What happens if I forget my password?

If you forget your password and cannot login, contact our support team with the form at the bottom of this page. We'll be happy to help you restore your account!

Can I have multiple accounts with the same email? The same username?

Unfortunately, we are unable to have more than one account for a given username or email. Your username is your unique identifier to other users, so it cannot be copied, and your email is your unique identifier within the system, so it must stay linked to one account.

What information is public?

Currently, all users will be able to see your name, username, and your goals. Your email and any other personal info will not be shared.

Goals

How many goals do I have to have?

As many as you want! There is no minimum or maximum number of goals to be a member of the Go-Goal community. Everyone starts somewhere, and even having no goals yet but joining for ideas is a wonderful place to start!

What if I don't have any goals?

Try to discover some! Friends and other users goals are public, so feel free to look around other pages and see if anything sparks interest. It may even be a bonding opportunity! Still not interested in anything you see? That's okay! You can always check back if there's anything you'd like to try.

What if I no longer want to do a goal?

You can easily go to the goals page and click the trash icon if you choose to delete any of your goals. This will make it no longer appear in any views of your profile, but will not add to your XP. You can always add back a deleted goal if you feel that you're now in a place to complete it.

When do my goals time out?

Never! Once you set a goal, you will have the opportunity to complete it so long as you don't choose to remove it. Go-Goal is about completing our objectives, not the amount of time it takes, so never feel pressured by time to finish something faster than you are comfortable with. Everyone has their own pace!

How do I gain XP?

XP is gained every time you mark a goal as completed. More XP will increase your level, which will be your way to show that you are on a roll with your personal goals! Some goals that take more effort may be worth more XP than simpler goals, but every completion will add something to your progress!

Friends

How do I send a friend request?

Once you're logged in, viewing any profile will have a button by their details allowing you to send a friend request. If you have already sent one, it will show that your request is pending, and if it's accepted it will label that user as your friend!

How do I accept a friend request?

When on your own profile page, a panel will show you any users who have sent you a friend request. You can take as much time as needed before accepting or declining, and can view their profile first to make sure tey are who you think they are. Once you've decided whether to accept or reject, there are two simple buttons next to their name on the pending panel for you to click to make your decision.

How do I find friends?

The discover tab allows you to search for any user if you know who you are looking for. Future updates will also pull up users with similar goals for you to meet over the app. Once you have some friends, you will also be able to view their friends on their profile, and hopefully make some mutual connections.

What if I don't have any friends on the site?

Feel free to make some! The discover tab is a great way to meet people with similar interests. You also can spread the word about our site so more people you know can keep tabs on their goals right here!
